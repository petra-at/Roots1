About Us

Established by salon owner Nana, Linda Boaitey in 1990.
Root1 has evolved to become one of the best Afro & European hair salons in Tooting, South London.
Root1 has been running for years!
Our secret to success is the provision of excellent customer care and quality hair service, for both men, women and kids alike.
We offer a range of cuts, colours and hairstyles that will leave you looking your best everytime.
If you've seen a hairstyle you like on a celebrity or want to try out a look you've found online our hairdressers are here to help.
